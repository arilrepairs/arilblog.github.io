This week I got more accustomed to working in an office environment where there are policies and procedures that need to be followed. Avoiding a shotgun approach helps accountability and efficiency, along with making teamwork more effective. A couple times this week a laptop was brought in with a usb-c issue. If these were my own laptops, I'd just take it to a hot air soldering station and replace the port itself. But these devices are under long-lasting warranties that even cover accidental damages, so they should just be repaired under that warranty (even though it would take longer than me just doing it myself). But I have more important work to do, and it would be a waste of mine and everyone else's time in this work setting.

Week in retrospect - Week of Monday, July 6th, 2026

Published: July 13, 2026

For the week of Monday, July 6th, 2026, I was finally exposed to how a real IT department operates. The most important things for IT is repeatability and accountability. That's the benefit of ticketing systems, is a way for IT employees to record their work. And because I am working for a healthcare company, I have also been exposed to the importance of Private Health Information (PHI), which is separate but similar to PII (Personal Identifiable Information). A few other concepts that have been reinforced over the course of my first week are warranties and inventory tracking, trust but verify (always make sure the person is who they say they are and what they want is actually what they need), and decomissioning and lifecycle management.
